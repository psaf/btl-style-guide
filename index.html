Our colours consist of professional, yet slightly warming, tones. Our primary tone is the purple. It should be the most prevalent on any material. Our secondary tone is the pink. It should be used to highlight important information, or add flare. Our tertiary tone is the teal. It should be used subtlely details to add life.

Logos

Our logo is very simple. h normal logo has all of the BTL colours, most prevalently the purple. It looks professional and very easy to add to many different kinds of marketing material, and websites. The source files are located on the dropbox.
